Help me create a Zettelkasten-style atomic note. An atomic note should:

1. **Contain one main idea** - Focus on a single concept or thought
2. **Be self-contained** - Understandable without reading other notes
3. **Have a unique identifier** - Use timestamp or sequential ID
4. **Include links** - Reference related notes with [[Note Name]]
5. **Be written in your own words** - Processed, not just copied
